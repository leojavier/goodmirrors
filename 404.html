Emerging Visionary Grant

Apply Now!

SheaMoisture, in partnership with Good Mirrors,

is continuing its commitment to invest in the dreams of Black womxn and celebrate the change makers who are creating history in the world today.

Grants will be awarded as ten (10) $5,000 grants.

The Emerging Visionary grant
is a $50,000 opportunity for artists
whose work is rooted in uplifting their community
for positive impact.

Criteria

Eligible creatives are those whose work has a community focus. Their work must be rooted in uplifting their community for positive impact.

• Must be a creative who is the process of building influence as a creator.

• Must showcase a portfolio of work that is rooted in uplifting your community.

• Have an existing platform with examples of community work (digital presence, community awards, partnerships programs, website or social media).
